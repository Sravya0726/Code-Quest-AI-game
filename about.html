About ORSA'25

ORSA'25 is a 2-day national-level techno-cultural fest hosted by Global Institute of Engineering and Technology. This event brings together the brightest minds in AI, Data Science, CSE, and core branches like Civil, Mechanical, and ECE.

The CodeQuest AI Game is one of the fest’s spotlight tech events. It’s a multi-round challenge combining riddles, AI logic, sequencing puzzles, programming, and a final AI debate or code challenge depending on department.
